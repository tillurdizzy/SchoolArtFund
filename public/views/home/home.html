Who We Are

School Arts Fund is a 501.C3 organization with a mission to provide much needed funding for public school arts programs. Our primary source of income is derived from our corporate partners and sponsors, along with sale of original artwork and other donated items from local and national artists. These funds will be used for traditional art programs such as drawing, painting, music, and drama, as well as "new media" technology programs including web design and 3D modeling. SAF also supports the industrial trade arts, such as metal shop and wood shop.
