Become a SAF Partner!

SAF Partners are a community of local businesses who encourage patronage among themselves. SAF publishes a quarterly booklet of partners, and hosts a bi-weekly Food Truck Party at partner locations. Becoming a SAF Partner is a great way to market your business, meet and network with other local business owners, and even get to know local city officials.
